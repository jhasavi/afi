# How to run your brokerage with AdvisorFlow

AdvisorFlow is not meant to replace Namaste Boston Mission Control, HubSpot, your phone, or your email inbox. It is the daily operating layer that tells you who to contact, why today, what to say, and what to do next.

## The simple stack

| Tool | Job |
| --- | --- |
| Namaste Boston Mission Control | Source of truth for clients, services, journeys, notes, tasks, and portal workflows |
| AdvisorFlow | Daily follow-up list, message drafts, business review, and outreach discipline |
| HubSpot | Newsletter list and campaign emails |
| Your phone/email | Final send channel for personal 1:1 outreach unless Team NB email send is configured |

## Daily rhythm

1. Open **Business review**.
2. Check the business health score, overdue count, stale warm relationships, and this week's moves.
3. Open **Today**.
4. Work the ranked list from top to bottom.
5. Generate or edit the draft.
6. Send from your own phone/email, or use **Send via NB mail** when Team email send is configured.
7. Click **Log as sent** or **Log as contacted** so the relationship record updates.

The goal is not to spend all day inside software. The goal is 15-20 minutes of consistent, high-quality relationship touches.

## Weekly review

Every Friday:

1. Open **Weekly review**.
2. Clear overdue contacts.
3. Pick 3 stale past clients to revive next week.
4. Pick 3 referral partners to touch next week.
5. Export or sync HubSpot if you added many new emails.
6. Check **Business review** again and write down the next week's focus.

## What to watch

| Metric | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Contacts touched in 30 days | Shows whether the relationship engine is alive |
| Overdue follow-ups | Shows where warm opportunities are leaking |
| Active opportunities | Shows near-term revenue focus |
| Stale warm relationships | Usually the easiest source of repeat/referral business |
| Missing email/phone | Limits outreach, HubSpot export, and sync quality |
| NB-linked contacts | Shows whether Mission Control and AdvisorFlow are working together |

## Recommended rules

- Work **Today** before noon.
- Touch past clients at least quarterly.
- Touch referral partners monthly.
- Do not auto-send cold or sensitive messages.
- Keep rich client notes in Mission Control.
- Keep AdvisorFlow focused on action, not record-keeping.

## First 7 days

Day 1: Import or sync contacts.

Day 2: Work Today and log at least 3 touches.

Day 3: Fix missing emails/phones for important relationships.

Day 4: Add estimated value to active opportunities.

Day 5: Export HubSpot CSV for newsletter cleanup.

Day 6: Review stale warm relationships.

Day 7: Open Business review and decide next week's focus.

## When the product is working

You should feel less dependent on memory. Warm leads should not disappear. Past clients should hear from you before they need another broker. Referral partners should get useful, timely notes. Mission Control remains organized, while AdvisorFlow creates the daily behavior that turns the database into business.
