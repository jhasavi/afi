# AdvisorFlow AI — Product Vision

## Mission

Help real estate and mortgage advisors **turn the relationships they already have into consistent business** — by making daily follow-up effortless, compliant, and human.

## North star

**AdvisorFlow AI** is the daily relationship operating system for advisors who lose deals not from lack of leads, but from **inconsistent follow-up**.

Every morning, an advisor should know exactly:

1. **Who** to contact
2. **Why today**
3. **What to say**
4. **What happens next**

— without the app sending anything on their behalf until they choose to.

## The problem

- CRMs store contacts but don't tell you who matters **today**
- Follow-up slips when you're busy with showings, closings, and referrals
- Generic templates feel robotic; blank pages feel overwhelming
- Compliance risk when AI over-promises on rates, appreciation, or fair housing

## Our solution

| Capability | What it does |
|------------|--------------|
| **Today's list** | Scores your book and surfaces the highest-value outreach (3–5 people by plan) |
| **Draft messages** | Warm, local, channel-specific copy you review and send yourself |
| **Advisory briefs** | Prep for conversations with likely needs, questions, and next steps |
| **Pipeline + dashboard** | Lightweight visibility without enterprise CRM complexity |
| **Draft-first safety** | No surprise emails or texts — ever |

## Outcome loop (product truth)

```
Score book → Today's list → Why today → Draft → You send → Log as sent → Next follow-up
```

If a feature doesn't strengthen that loop, it is secondary.

## Personas

### Jordan — Solo Realtor
- 200+ past clients and leads in a spreadsheet
- Wants 15 minutes each morning to stay top-of-mind
- Success: contacts their daily list with confidence

### Maria — Loan Officer
- Referral partners and refinance leads go cold
- Needs compliant language, not rate quotes
- Success: systematic partner nurture without compliance anxiety

### Avery Group — Small team (Team plan)
- Shared CRM / Mission Control as source of truth
- Needs import + write-back of touch dates, not another CRM
- Success: no warm lead goes quiet because nobody followed up

## Go-to-market wedge

1. **Customer zero:** Namaste Boston advisors on Team (Mission Control sync + write-back)
2. **Public solo:** Free → Solo Pro trial for CSV-first independents
3. **Later:** brokerage white-label after retention proof

Prove the daily loop with a real CRM integration before scaling generic SEO/content.

## Principles

1. **Draft-first forever** — copy/paste is the default
2. **Advisor-grade AI** — warm, specific, compliant; templates always available
3. **Trust through transparency** — show scores, AI mode, plan limits, and assumptions
4. **Plan honesty** — Free never quietly uses paid AI; entitlements enforced server-side
5. **Integrations behind flags** — sync and draft, never auto-send

## Success metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Daily active advisors completing Today's list | 60%+ of weekly actives |
| Drafts generated per user per week | 10+ |
| Log-as-sent rate (drafts actually used) | 40%+ |
| Time to first Today's list after signup | < 5 minutes |
| Week-4 weekly retention (pilot) | 60%+ |

## Differentiation

- **Not a CRM replacement** — a daily action layer on top of your book
- **Not an auto-dialer** — human judgment stays in the loop
- **Not generic ChatGPT** — scoring, guardrails, and real-estate context built in
